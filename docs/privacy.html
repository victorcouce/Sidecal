Sidecal — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data Collection

Sidecal does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API.

What We Store Locally

  • Your selected favorite YouTube channels (IDs, names, avatars)
  • Cached video publication dates from public YouTube RSS feeds
  • Computed upload predictions
  • Your extension preferences

External Requests

Sidecal only communicates with youtube.com to read publicly available RSS feeds and your subscription list. No data is sent to any third-party server.

No Tracking

Sidecal does not use analytics, telemetry, cookies, or any form of tracking.

Permissions

  • storage: Save your favorites and preferences locally
  • alarms: Schedule periodic data refresh
  • host_permissions (youtube.com): Integrate with YouTube's interface and read public RSS feeds

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
